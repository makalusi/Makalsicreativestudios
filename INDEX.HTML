Case Study
Web Design Brand Identity Nairobi · 2024

DELPHI
GRAPHICS

A full web redesign and brand consistency project for Nairobi's go-to print and branding house — built to convert visitors into clients.

01 / The Brief

Print Sharp.
Brand Boldly.

Delphi Graphics had the craft — years of delivering premium printing, signage, apparel, and event branding across Nairobi. What they needed was a digital presence that matched it. They came to Makalsi with one clear goal: a website that does the selling for them.

02 / The Challenge

Three things were holding them back.

01

A buried service catalogue

Stationery, signage, apparel, merchandise, event branding — all hidden in a layout that made it hard to find what you needed.

02

No clear path to action

Visitors landed, scrolled, and left. There was no obvious next step — no strong CTA, no enquiry flow designed to convert interest into a conversation.

03

Brand inconsistency online

Their printed work was sharp and professional. Their digital presence wasn't. The gap was costing them credibility at first glance.

03 / Our Approach

We started with the why,
not the pixels.

Before a single frame was designed, we ran a full UX and brand audit. We mapped how real users navigate a print services site, identified where the drop-offs were, and restructured the entire information architecture around one goal: make it effortless to understand what Delphi does and easy to get in touch.

Phase 01

Discovery & Audit

UX review · service mapping · competitor analysis

Phase 02

Architecture & Strategy

Site structure · user journeys · CTA planning

Phase 03

Design & Build

UI design · responsive development · brand system

Phase 04

Launch & Optimise

Performance tuning · SEO · go-live

04 / Deliverables

Everything they needed.
Nothing they didn't.

Full website redesign — mobile-first, fully responsive
Structured service pages: Print · Apparel · Signage · Merch · Events
Enquiry-optimised contact and quote request flow
Brand consistency system carried through every page
Customer testimonial section — 4.9 / 5 social proof
Lighthouse performance score 95+ · fast load · SEO-ready

05 / The Outcome

A site that works as hard
as the team behind it.

95+

Lighthouse Score

4.9/5

Client Rating

100%

Mobile Optimised

Delphi Graphics now has a digital home that reflects the premium quality of their work — clear, bold, and built to convert. Every visitor knows exactly what they offer and exactly how to reach them.

E-commerce Brand Identity Kenya · 2024

ROLLING
TOTAL
FITNESS

A high-performance e-commerce build for Kenya's premium supplements brand — 160+ products, nationwide delivery, and a conversion engine built to fuel gains.

01 / The Brief

Quality you can trust.
Results you can feel.

Rolling Total Fitness had the products — lab-tested proteins, pre-workouts, vitamins, and weight management supplements trusted by athletes across Kenya. What they needed was an e-commerce platform that matched their ambition: one that could handle a growing catalogue of 160+ SKUs, drive conversions, and position them as the premium choice in a competitive supplement market.

02 / The Challenge

160+ products.
Zero clear structure.

01

A catalogue without architecture

Protein, pre-workout, vitamins, weight management — 160+ products across four categories with no browsing structure that made sense to a customer in a hurry.

02

No brand identity in a crowded market

The supplement space in Kenya is noisy. Without a distinct visual identity and strong trust signals, Rolling was being lost in a sea of generic competitors.

03

Promotions not converting

Strong offers — Buy 2 Get 1 Free, first-order discounts — were buried or absent entirely. The commercial ammunition was there; the site just wasn't firing it.

03 / Our Approach

Built like an athlete.
Optimised like a machine.

We treated the site build like a training programme — structured, purposeful, and tracked. Every design decision was tied to a commercial outcome: increase average order value, reduce drop-off, and build the trust needed to convert a first-time visitor into a repeat customer.

Phase 01

Product Architecture

Catalogue audit · category structure · filtering logic

Phase 02

Brand & Visual Identity

Athletic aesthetic · colour system · trust badge design

Phase 03

E-commerce Build

Product pages · promotions engine · checkout flow · mobile-first

Phase 04

Launch & CRO

Conversion rate optimisation · performance tuning · Kenya SEO

04 / Deliverables

Every feature
earned its place.

Full e-commerce website — mobile-first, fully responsive
160+ products across 4 structured categories
Promotions engine — Buy 2 Get 1 Free, first-order discount (ROLL20)
Trust signal system — lab-tested badges, 30-day returns, 24hr shipping
Nationwide Kenya delivery integration — free shipping over KShs 75
Athletic brand identity system — bold, high-contrast, gym-culture aesthetic
Kenya e-commerce SEO — optimised for supplement searches nationwide

05 / The Outcome

A store that trains
as hard as its customers.

160+

Products Live

4

Categories Built

24hr

Shipping Nationwide

Rolling Total Fitness launched with a store that could scale with them — clear product navigation, active promotions driving average order value, and a brand identity that stands out in Kenya's crowded supplement market.

Restaurant Web Brand Identity Nairobi · 2024

RED
GINGER

A full website redesign for Nairobi's premier Pan-Asian dining experience — built to put the food front and centre and turn browsers into diners.

01 / The Brief

Bold flavours.
Asian craft.

Red Ginger had the cuisine, the atmosphere, and the reputation. What they lacked was a digital home that matched the experience of walking through their doors. Serving Japanese, Thai, Vietnamese, and Korean dishes from 2nd Parklands Avenue, they needed a site that made people hungry before they even arrived.

02 / The Challenge

Great food deserves
a great first impression.

01

No visual storytelling

The existing presence didn't showcase the food, the atmosphere, or the craft behind the kitchen. Visitors couldn't feel the experience before visiting.

02

Menu buried and hard to browse

16+ signature sushi rolls, small plates, and large plates across four Asian cuisines — all hidden behind poor navigation that left diners guessing.

03

Private events not being captured

Date nights, proposals, and group bookings — high-value opportunities with no dedicated page, no enquiry flow, and no way for the site to do the selling.

03 / Our Approach

Food-first.
Conversion-always.

We designed around the dining journey — from the moment someone searches for a restaurant to the moment they make a booking. Every section was built to reduce friction and increase appetite.

Phase 01

Discovery & Dining Journey Mapping

User research · competitor dining sites · menu architecture

Phase 02

Visual Strategy

Food photography hierarchy · colour direction · typography

Phase 03

Design & Development

UI design · mobile-first build · menu pages · events section

Phase 04

Launch & SEO

Performance tuning · local SEO · Nairobi dining search optimisation

04 / Deliverables

Everything a restaurant
website needs to work.

Full restaurant website redesign — mobile-first, fully responsive
Full menu showcase: 16+ sushi rolls, small plates, large plates
Private events & group bookings section
Date night & proposal package pages
Reservation and contact flow optimised for conversion
Brand photography integration — food-first visual hierarchy
Local SEO optimised for Nairobi restaurant searches

05 / The Outcome

A site that makes people
hungry before they arrive.

4+

Mobile Optimised

Red Ginger now has a digital presence as bold as their flavours — a site that tells the story, showcases the menu, and captures reservations and private event enquiries without a phone call needed.
